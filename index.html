Arthur has been a musician ever since a young child. Starting on the piano at age 5 and later on moving to guitar, drums and 6-string bass. Mostly self taught from reading books and learning from friends he became very interested in music theory and began to teach at a local music school.

Since the age of 18 Arthur has been in many original and cover bands playing a variety of instruments. He has let music become everything and consumed his entire life. He has been playing for many years all around South Florida as his sole source of income. Lately Arthur has been getting a little sick of that scene ;) and needs a change of pace. Now he studies computer programming at University of Miami in hopes to take over the world (or at least the music industry) with his newly formed coding skills!
